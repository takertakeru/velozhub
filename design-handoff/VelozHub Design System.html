Design System

VelozHub

A small, utilitarian system for a shared-car schedule. Neutral chrome keeps the interface calm and high-contrast for outdoor use, while five fixed person-colors do the heavy lifting: at a glance you know whose trip is whose. Built mobile-first and adapted up, not stretched.

01

Color

Every family member owns one color, used on their bookings everywhere: dot, chip, avatar, agenda rail, and week block. Hues were chosen to stay distinct for color-vision differences and to hold contrast in both themes.

Typography

One typeface, Hanken Grotesk, a humanist grotesk that is friendly but crisp. Tabular figures are used for all times so columns of clock values stay aligned. On 1920-class screens nothing drops below 14px; tap targets never below 44px.
